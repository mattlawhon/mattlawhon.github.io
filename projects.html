Reimagine the clock face
This was my first javascript design. Using the p5.js library, this design switches the traditional channel for reading a clock, the angles of the clock hands, to the radii of the three circles representing hour, minute and second. Concentric circles mark every hour and every five minutes and seconds to facilitate quick reading.
